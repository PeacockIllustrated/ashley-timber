Our Story

Founded in South Shields over three decades ago, Ashley Timber started with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality timber and building materials with the friendly, expert service our community deserves. We grew from a small local yard to the largest timber merchant in the area by sticking to that promise.

We are proud to be an independent, family-run business. Our roots in the North East run deep, and we are committed to serving and supporting the local tradespeople, DIY enthusiasts, and builders who have trusted us for generations.
